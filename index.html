Process

Four points of light

Every project follows the same constellation — a clear path from first conversation to final delivery.

  1. Discover

    We talk about your business, your audience, and what you need the content to do. Plain conversation — no creative briefs required.

  2. Create

    Origyn produces your content using professional AI tools and a studio eye — most packages deliver in days, not weeks.

  3. Refine

    You review everything. One revision is included with every deliverable, so the final version says exactly what you want it to.

  4. Deliver

    You get finished files sized for where they'll live — social feeds, your website, print, or all three.
